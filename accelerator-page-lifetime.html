The Acquisition Journey

Your Path from Search to Close

Acquira is designed to help you close as fast as possible. Put your all into it and you could close your first deal faster than you think — here's how the program supports you every step of the way.

🔎

Step 1: The Search

Define your investment thesis. Set up broker profiles, activate your ClickUp deal flow, and begin evaluating on-market opportunities that match your criteria.

📝

Step 2: Deal Review

Present qualified deals to Acquira's expert team. Receive structured feedback on deal quality, pricing, and your thesis — and keep refining your search until you find the right one.

🏨

Step 3: Ascend Workshop

An invite-only, multi-day live workshop for members who are actively searching and have reached key program milestones. Acquira covers accommodations and meals.

📂

Step 4: Due Diligence

Dig into the financials with Acquira's calculators, checklists, and AI tools. Use your coaching sessions to pressure-test your analysis and get expert eyes on the deal before you're committed.

🔑

Step 5: Closing

Prepare your SBA financing package, work with closing attorneys, finalize the purchase agreement, and take ownership of your business. Acquira's team is with you through the finish line.

📈

Step 6: Stabilize & Grow

Transition into ownership with the ACE Management Framework — Acquira's post-close operating system for stabilizing operations and building toward growth.

This is a DIY process — and the timeline is yours to own. Some members have closed deals within 3 months of joining. For a buyer starting from square one, the goal is ownership in under a year. And if a deal falls through? You keep working the program — there's no clock running against you.
